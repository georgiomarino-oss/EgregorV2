Produce truthful, launch-ready store materials that match the current product, compliance surfaces, and operational readiness.

## ASO Rules

- Base every claim on current repo truth, not aspirational roadmap.
- Keep screenshot stories grounded in the canonical product:
  - Home / Solo
  - Circles
  - Live
  - Profile / Settings
- Highlight trust, clarity, and premium atmosphere without making unverifiable promises.
- Align deletion, privacy, support, and subscription wording with the web surfaces users and reviewers will actually see.

## Never Overstate

- push delivery maturity
- iOS release readiness
- social auth availability
- event-audio readiness
- moderation-ops maturity
- anything not supported by current runtime or evidence

## Screenshot Guidance

- Show real canonical screens and states, not invented concepts.
- Keep captions concrete and user-facing.
- Include trust or control surfaces where relevant, especially for reviewers.
- Avoid screenshots that depend on removed legacy flows.

## Deliverables

- listing title/subtitle/short description/full description options
- keyword or metadata suggestions appropriate to the target store
- screenshot/caption plan
- reviewer notes and open-risk list

## Pairing Guidance

- Pair with `$release-readiness-qa` for launch truth and blocker review.
- Pair with `$trust-safety-compliance` whenever privacy, deletion, moderation, or public claims are involved.
